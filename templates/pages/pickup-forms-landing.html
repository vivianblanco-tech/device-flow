Single Laptop Shipment: Use this for individual laptop requests. The laptop will be picked up from your location, processed at our warehouse, and delivered to the engineer.
Bulk Shipment: Use this for shipping multiple laptops at once. All laptops will be stored at the warehouse until they're assigned to engineers.
