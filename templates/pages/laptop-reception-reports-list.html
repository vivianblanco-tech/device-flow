About Reception Reports

Reception reports are created by warehouse users when they receive individual laptops. Each report documents:

  • Receipt date and time
  • Three required photos: serial number, external condition, and working condition
  • Condition notes and observations
  • Approval status - must be approved by logistics before laptop becomes available

Reports with "Pending Approval" status need to be reviewed and approved by logistics users.
