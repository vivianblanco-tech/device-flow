About Reception Reports

Reception reports document the receipt of individual laptops at the warehouse. After submission:

  • A notification will be sent to international.logistics@bairesdev.com
  • The report will be pending approval from logistics
  • Once approved, the laptop status will change to "Available at Warehouse"
  • The laptop can then be assigned to software engineers
