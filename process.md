Step 1: Logistics recevies a request for hardware deployment
Step 2: Logistics sends magic link to Client pickup form to the Project Manager. At the same time, requests that the Software Engineer confirm his/her valid address via email.
Step 3: The Project Manager forwards the link to the client
Step 4: The Client fills out the pickup form. An automatic email is sent to the Client confirming that the pickup is being processed.
Step 5: Logistics creates the courier labels and sends them to the Client along with the estimated pickup time and instructions.
Step 6: The Client prints out the labels and attaches them to the shipment. The shipment is picked up by the courier. 
Step 7: Logistics confirms the pickup and notifies the Warehouse about the expected shipment arrival. (Pre-alert email) Dashboard and Calendar is updated to show in transit to Warehouse status.
Step 8: The Warehouse receives the shipment and notifies Logistics, creates a Reception Report and uploads photos of the shipment. The Dashboard is updated to show the shipment has arrived at the Warehouse.
Step 9: Logistics arranges pickup from the Warehouse for delivery to the Software Engineer. (Release hardware email).
Step 10: Warehouse confirms the pickup via email. Dashboard is updated to show shipment in-transit status.
Step 11: The Software Engineer receives the shipment and takes photos, fills out the Delivery Form.
Step 12: Dashboard and Calendar is updated to show delivered status.
